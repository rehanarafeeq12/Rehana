The School's annual day is a culmination of the school's academic year. It's a time for celebration and recognisation of the students as well as the school's achievement.The annual day function is considered to be a red-letter day for every school. It's a very special day for the management of the school, students, teachers and parents. On this day the school management shares it's vision and future plans whereas students perfom various cultural activities. Celebrating school's annual day provides the school with an opportunity to showcase it's achievements..
